Worksheet Prompts

  1. Write the next three due dates.
  2. List the spending leak that can be paused this week.
  3. Write the smallest income follow-up to do next.
  4. Name the bill or purchase that must not surprise you.

Pack Checklist

  1. Three due dates visible
  2. One pause candidate picked
  3. One income follow-up named
  4. No investment or trading advice added
  5. Next cash decision written
