Listing Photo Checklist

A practical shot list for creating clearer marketplace, rental, or product listing photos.

Open worksheet Open checklist Store link not connected

Who This Helps

People preparing simple listings who need better photos without hiring a photographer.

Worksheet Prompts

  1. List the item, room, or product being photographed.
  2. Take one wide shot, one detail shot, and one scale shot.
  3. Write what defect or limitation must be shown honestly.
  4. Choose the best cover image.

Pack Checklist

  1. Wide shot taken
  2. Detail shot taken
  3. Scale shot taken
  4. Limitations shown honestly
  5. Cover image chosen
