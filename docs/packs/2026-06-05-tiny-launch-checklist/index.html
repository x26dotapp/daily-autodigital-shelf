Product FAQ

Is Tiny Launch Checklist a paid checkout?

No. The pack download is public while product checkout remains disconnected.

How can someone support this pack?

Use the support action for this pack. The branded support-intent redirect goes to the external Square support page with pack attribution.

What is included in the download?

The ZIP includes a printable worksheet, checklist, cover image, seller copy, manifest, and related pack files.

Support links route to a Square-hosted CalmSprout gift-card/support page. Product checkout is not connected yet; pack downloads remain public until a store is connected.
